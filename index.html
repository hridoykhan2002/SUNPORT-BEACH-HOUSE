Activities

Nestled into the sands that stretch along the bay, The Sunport Beach Club has it’s own private beach on a resort like setting with sun loungers and umbrellas. Soak up the sun and surf at this family resort and enjoy all of the non-motorized water equipment which includes Peddle boats, Tubes, Kayaks, Stand up paddle boards and more. When you are ready to change it up a bit you can visit some of the multiple attractions in the area. There is something for the entire family. Go karting, Mini golf, Boat Charters, Historical sites and Golf are all within 15 minutes of the Sunport Beach Club.
